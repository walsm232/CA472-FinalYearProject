About Us

Welcome to Student Network

We're a social networking platform connecting students accross Ireland and providing them with access to valuable resources enabling them to excel in college.

We want to help students in colleges and universities accross Ireland get the most out their time in college!

Our Goal

Before the development of this platform there was no dedicated student social networking platform on the market!

We believed that a platform was needed for students to interact, share, discuss and debate with each other.

We want to focus on allowing students to create valuable real world connections with the hopes of building meaningful friendships.
